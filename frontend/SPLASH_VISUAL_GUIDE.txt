ANIMATION SEQUENCE:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

0ms     ┃ Native splash appears (instant)
        ┃ • Black background
        ┃ • Your logo (if provided)
        ┃
100ms   ┃ Flutter engine loads
        ┃
200ms   ┃ Flutter splash widget appears
        ┃ • Fade in starts (opacity 0 → 1)
        ┃ • Scale animation starts (0.8 → 1.0)
        ┃
600ms   ┃ Fade in complete
        ┃ • Logo fully visible
        ┃ • Text fully visible
        ┃
1500ms  ┃ Animation complete
        ┃ • Loading spinner visible
        ┃
2000ms  ┃ Minimum display time reached
        ┃ • Initialization complete
        ┃ • Transition to main app
        ┃
2100ms  ┃ Main app visible
        ┃ • Feed screen or Login screen
